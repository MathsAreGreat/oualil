Développer à l’aide des identités remarquables

$A=\pbig{\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{5}}^2$

$B=\pbig{\sqrt{2}+3\sqrt{5}}^2$

$C=\pbig{\sqrt{2}+\sqrt{3}}\pbig{\sqrt{2}-\sqrt{3}}$

$D=\pbig{\sqrt{5}-\sqrt{2}}^2$

$E=\pbig{2\sqrt{5}+3\sqrt{2}}^2$

$F=\pbig{3\sqrt{2}-3}\pbig{3\sqrt{2}+3}$

$G=\pbig{3\sqrt{2}+1}^2$

$H=\pbig{2\sqrt{3}-1}\pbig{2\sqrt{3}+1}$

$I=\pbig{2\sqrt{5}-3\sqrt{2}}^2$

$J=\pbig{\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2}}\pbig{\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2}}$

$\begin{aligned} A&=\left(\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{5}\right)^2 \\ &=\left(\sqrt{3}\right)^2-2\sqrt{3}\times \sqrt{5}+\left(\sqrt{5}\right)^2 \\ &=3-2\sqrt{15}+5 \\ &=8-2\sqrt{15} \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned}B&=\left(\sqrt{2}+3\sqrt{5}\right)^2 \\ &=\left(\sqrt{2}\right)^2+2\sqrt{2}\times 3\sqrt{5}+\left(3\sqrt{5}\right)^2 \\ &=2+6\sqrt{10}+9\times 5 \\ &=2+6\sqrt{10}+45 \\ &=47+6\sqrt{10} \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned}C&=\left(\sqrt{2}+\sqrt{3}\right)\left(\sqrt{2}-\sqrt{3}\right) \\ &=\left(\sqrt{2}\right)^2-\left(\sqrt{3}\right)^2 \\ &=2-3 \\ &=-1 \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned} D&=\left(\sqrt{5}-\sqrt{2}\right)^2 \\ &=\left(\sqrt{5}\right)^2-2\sqrt{5}\times \sqrt{2}+\left(\sqrt{2}\right)^2 \\ &=5-2\sqrt{10}+2 \\ &=7-2\sqrt{10} \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned} E&=\left(2\sqrt{5}+3\sqrt{2}\right)^2 \\ &=\left(2\sqrt{5}\right)^2+2\times 2\sqrt{5}\times 3\sqrt{2}+\left(3\sqrt{2}\right)^2 \\ &=4 \times 5+12\sqrt{10}+9\times 2 \\ &=20+12\sqrt{10}+18 \\ &=38+12\sqrt{10} \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned}F&=\left(3\sqrt{2}-3\right)\left(3\sqrt{2}+3\right) \\ &=\left(3\sqrt{2}\right)^2-9 \\ &=9\times 2-9 \\ &=18-9\\ &=9 \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned} G&=\left(3\sqrt{2}+1\right)^2 \\ &=\left(3\sqrt{2}\right)^2+2\times 3\sqrt{2}\times 1+1^2 \\ &=9 \times 2+6\sqrt{2}+1 \\ &=18+6\sqrt{2}+1 \\ &=19+6\sqrt{2} \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned} H&=\left(2\sqrt{3}-1\right)\left(2\sqrt{3}+1\right) \\ &=\left(2\sqrt{3}\right)^2-1^2 \\ &=4\times 3-1\\ &=12-1\\ &=11 \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned}I&=\left(2\sqrt{5}-3\sqrt{2}\right)^2 \\ &=\left(2\sqrt{5}\right)^2-2\times 2\sqrt{5}\times 3\sqrt{2}+\left(3\sqrt{2}\right)^2 \\ &=4\times 5-12\sqrt{10}+9\times 2 \\ &=20-12\sqrt{10}+18\\ &=38-12\sqrt{10} \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
$\begin{aligned} J&=\left(\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2}\right)\left(\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2}\right) \\ &=\left(\sqrt{3}\right)^2-\left(\sqrt{2}\right)^2 \\ &=3-2\\ &=1 \end{aligned}$ $\quad$
